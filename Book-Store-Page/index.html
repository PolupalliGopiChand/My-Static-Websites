Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

"Wings of Fire" is the inspirational autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President of India. It takes readers on a journey through his early life, the struggles he faced in his pursuit of knowledge, and his successful career as a scientist. Kalam’s reflections and lessons about leadership, resilience, and the importance of education resonate deeply with readers of all ages.

The 3 Mistakes of My Life

by Chetan Bhagat

"The 3 Mistakes of My Life" is a captivating novel by Chetan Bhagat that weaves a story of love, friendship, and ambition. The story follows three friends as they face challenges in their personal and professional lives. The book delves into their struggles and mistakes, ultimately revealing how they overcome them, delivering a powerful message about the importance of second chances.

Harry Potter

by J.K.Rowling

"Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" is the first book in J.K. Rowling’s iconic fantasy series. It introduces the young wizard, Harry Potter, as he embarks on an adventure at the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The book is filled with magic, wonder, and a sense of adventure, capturing the imaginations of both children and adults. It’s a story about friendship, bravery, and the triumph of good over evil.
